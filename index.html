Lair of the Feastbringer

A once-per-year 40-player social raid tuned for maximum emotional damage and Well Fed uptime.

Instance Type: Holiday Raid (Family)

Difficulty: High – mechanically simple, socially lethal

Recommended Size: 10–40 players

Primary Rewards: Well Fed, Leftovers, Parental Approval

Thanksgiving is a single-boss raid centered around The Feastbringer (Mom). Once Dinner Phase begins, the instance cannot be reset.

Attunement

Entry requires the questline What Are You Bringing?

  • Bring one dish, dessert, or drink.
  • Equip at least one polite greeting.
  • Acknowledge The Feastbringer upon arrival.

Failure applies Showed Up Empty-Handed, increasing Disappointment damage taken.

  • Grandma’s Meal: Best Well Fed buff in the game. Cannot be dispelled.
  • Cozy Home (Fireplace Aura): +40 stamina, reduces emotional damage.
  • Sibling Coordination: Improves interrupt coverage.
  • Good Night’s Sleep: Mandatory for progression pulls.

Greeting Trash Gauntlet

Before reaching the boss, clear the unskippable Greeting Gauntlet, filled with high-HP NPCs linked by social tethers.

Key Trash NPCs

Aunt Cluster

  • Hug Spam: Harmless but roots target briefly.
  • How’s Work? Roots target for 30s with forced dialogue.

Uncle of Infinite Stories

  • Story From ’78: Uninterruptible 90s monologue.
  • Misremembered Anecdote: Confuses target, lowering mental clarity.

Cousin Level 12

  • Casts Look What I Can Do repeatedly.

Neighbor Nobody Knows

  • Opens with I Don’t Believe We’ve Met.
  • Applies raid-wide awkwardness debuff.
Trash Tip: Absolutely avoid triggering Family Politics. Instant wipe.

Boss: The Feastbringer (Mom)

“She Who Must Be Obeyed” – Family, Humanoid

Health: Very High

Enrage Timer: Variable (behavior-based)

Wipe Conditions: Arguing, insufficient eating, refusing seconds

Immunities: Silence, hard CC, weak excuses

Global Abilities

  • Guilt Aura: Stacks on players who skip mechanics.
  • Overcooked Disappointment: Raid-wide emotional damage.
  • Plate Check: Random inspection; may trigger Guilt Bomb.

Active Abilities

  • Turkey Carve (Frontal Cleave): MT only; melee risk Dryness stacks.
  • Seconds Call: If < 50% accept seconds, triggers Disappointment Volley.
  • Gravy Slick: Ground effect causing slips and spills.
  • Dessert Enrage: Repeated Try My Pie casts.

Phase Breakdown

Phase 1: Table Setup

Soft RP phase. Minimal damage but high early-pull risk.

  • Do not hover in the kitchen.
  • Assign utility players to place dishes.
  • Ranged position near exits to kite conversation adds.

Phase 2: Main Course

Primary progression check; longest and most demanding.

  • Avoid You Didn’t Try My Dish stacks.
  • Healers manage physical and emotional damage.
  • Tanks rotate cooldowns during Carve + Seconds overlap.

Phase 3: Dessert (Soft Enrage)

  • Pie Beam: Chain-dessert effect.
  • Sweetness Overload: Severe slow.
  • Food Coma: Ticking incapacitation debuff.

Execute phase begins at ~85% fullness.

Hard Enrage: Political Discussion

If any NPC mentions politics, it’s over.

  • Immediate Subject Change can delay it (low success).

Phase 4: Cleanup (Hidden Final Boss)

Upon defeat, Mom summons The Sink, a high-armor stationary add.

  • Requires 5–7 cleaners.
  • Ignoring it applies Reputation Loss.
  • Completion grants Parental Approval.

Loot & Rewards

Common Drops

  • Leftovers: Multiple containers; quality scales with participation.
  • Well Fed Buff: Long-duration stat increase.
  • Ziplock Containers (BoE): Tradeable, reusable.

Rare Drops

  • Home Baked Rolls: High-value comfort food.
  • Pie Slice of Unusual Size: Cosmetic + morale.
  • Unattainable Warmth of Family: Nostalgia trinket.

Legendary Rewards

  • Take Some With You: Guarantees Leftover drop.
  • Mom’s Good Graces: Reputation buff until Christmas reset.

Achievements

  • [No Wipes]: No arguments.
  • [Seconds Heroic]: Accept seconds for all major dishes.
  • [The Pie-tanical]: Survive Dessert without Food Coma.
  • [Uncle Interruptor]: Stop Story From ’78 early.
  • [Full Clear]: Participate in cleanup unasked.

Lockout

Thanksgiving has a strict annual lockout (~365 days). Progress carries forward.
